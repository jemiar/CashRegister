Welcome to the Cash Register Application. Below are the commands supported by the app:
1. show
    - Display the total amount money in the register and the number of banknotes of $20, $10, $5, $2 and $1 respectively
2. put a1 a2 a3 a4 a5
    - ai >= 0 and ai is integer
    - Put ai amount of banknotes in $20, $10, $5, $2 and $1 respectively
3. take a1 a2 a3 a4 a5
    - ai >= 0 and ai is integer
    - Take out ai amount of banknotes in $20, $10, $5, $2 and $1 respectively
4. change a
    - a > 0 and a is integer
    - Change a amount of money. Return if banknotes if possible else return sorry
5. help
    - Display instructions to use the app
6. quit
    - Quit the app
